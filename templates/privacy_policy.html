Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 23, 2026

At EduPrompt, protecting your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information while using our Grammar Checker and other AI-powered tools.

1. Scope & Site Status

Our platform is live and available to users for AI-powered grammar checking. While we continue to enhance functionality, we are committed to maintaining a secure and privacy-respecting environment.

2. Information We Collect

To provide the best experience, EduPrompt may collect:

  • Basic contact details (e.g., email address) for account creation and verification
  • Usage data (e.g., number of words checked, tool usage frequency) to improve services
  • Preferences (e.g., selected language, document type)

We do not sell or share your personal information without your consent.

3. Why We Collect Data

Your information is used to:

  • Ensure accurate and personalized grammar checking
  • Enhance the functionality of the Grammar Checker
  • Provide responsive support and updates

4. Cookies & Tracking

EduPrompt may use cookies and similar technologies to improve usability and performance. Users can control or disable cookies via browser settings.

5. Security

We implement modern security practices including encryption, authentication, and restricted access to protect your information while using our Grammar Checker.

6. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our services or regulatory requirements. Users will be notified of significant updates.
